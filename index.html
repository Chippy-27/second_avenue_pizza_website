Welcome to Second Avenue Pizza, where every pizza is a fresh, handcrafted creation made just for you. We're not a franchise churning out identical pizzas — we're your neighbors, dedicated to bringing you the freshest ingredients and the most flavorful combinations in Sandpoint, Idaho.

From our legendary 7-pound Juke Box Special to our carefully crafted specialty pizzas, every bite tells a story of passion and quality. Come visit us on Second Avenue, grab a slice, and experience pizza that's truly out of this world.
